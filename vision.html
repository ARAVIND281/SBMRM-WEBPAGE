SRI BALAJI MODERN RICE MILL

MISSION

We believe in ensuring excellent quality of rice to reach each of our customers, and that remains our prime mission. We procure our grains directly from farmers across Tamil Nadu, freshly every season. The region where the rice is cultivated influences the taste and quality of the rice. Our rice is processed using most modern technology to retain some healthy bran. High bran content in Par Boiled rice makes it rich in Vitamin E.

VISION

Rice is a leading staple food. South India is one of the biggest markets that consume large quantities of rice. We work towards sustaining our legacy of quality and taste to deliver rice to every household in South India at the best price. We hope to make a healthy South India in our humble ways.

KEY OBJECTIVES

  • Hygienically processed rice products from source to customer.
  • Employs the latest in technology to ensure the best quality and elevated production rate.
  • A trusted name with satisfied customers all across South India.
  • Grains sourced from select farmers across India to maintain quality standards.

SRI BALAJI MODERN RICE MILL

About Us

Sri Balaji Modern Rice Mill is a pioneer in agro product manufacture , is one of the leading agricultural product manufacturers in the country The entire plant is spread over 3.5 acres of land. It was the first modern rice mill running in solar power in the entire state. It was the Pioneer in Double Boiled Rice (Kuruva) which was marketed specially to Kerala. It also specialized in Single Boiled Rice ,Steam Rice, Raw Rice.
We have been serving the people of South India with different rice varieties for generations, catering to strict standards of taste and expectations. Sri Balaji Modern Rice Mill products brings you some of the finest rice varieties of South India..With a state-of-the-art production facility in Tiruvannamalai in Tamil Nadu, Sri Balaji Modern Rice Mill produces over 40 tons per day of rice in 10 varieties. Catering to the different needs of varied clients. Our production facilities provide a livelihood to over 50 employees, each of whom plays an integral part in production and delivery of best quality taste & nourishment to our customers. We remain committed to deliver the highest quality of produce, while implement practices that benefit each of our stakeholders.
